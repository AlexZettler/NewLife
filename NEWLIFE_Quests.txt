
Every NPC has a home where they interact on a day to day basis


If an NPC requires to leave their home, it is considered a quest

Quests cover a broad spectrum and includes
    collecting food
    gainig happiness
    gaining wealth
    
Quest rewards are given upon actions of the quest being completed 








Need     ---> Desire
Interest ---> Desire ---> Actions
